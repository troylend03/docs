# Download Your Data

You have the right to access your personal data. Here's how to request and download a copy of everything Rallies stores about you.

## What's Included

Your data export includes:

### Account Information
- Profile details (name, email, settings)
- Account creation date
- Subscription history

### Usage Data
- AI conversation history
- Search history
- Feature usage logs

### Investment Data
- Watchlists and stocks tracked
- Price alerts you've set
- Portfolio holdings (if added)
- Performance history

### Preferences
- Notification settings
- Display preferences
- Connected accounts

## How to Request Your Data

### On Web

1. Go to **Settings → Account** or **Settings → Privacy**
2. Scroll to **"Your Data"** or **"Data Export"**
3. Click **"Request Data Export"**
4. Confirm your request

### Processing Time

- **Typical:** 24-48 hours
- **Maximum:** 7 days for complex requests
- **Notification:** You'll receive an email when ready

## Downloading Your Data

Once your export is ready:

1. You'll receive an email notification
2. Click the link in the email (valid for 7 days)
3. Or go to **Settings → Your Data → Download**
4. Click **"Download"** to get your files

## Export Format

Your data is provided as:

- **Format:** ZIP file containing JSON and/or CSV files
- **Size:** Varies based on your usage
- **Readable:** Can be opened in any text editor or spreadsheet

### File Structure

```
rallies-data-export/
├── account.json         # Profile and settings
├── conversations.json   # AI chat history
├── watchlists.csv       # Your watchlists
├── alerts.csv           # Price alerts
├── portfolio.csv        # Holdings (if applicable)
└── activity.json        # Usage history
```

## Data Retention

When you request your data:

- **Export available for 7 days** - Download before it expires
- **Original data unchanged** - Export doesn't delete anything
- **Request again anytime** - No limit on data requests

## Using Your Exported Data

Your data is yours to use however you want:

- **Personal records** - Keep for your own reference
- **Transfer** - Move to another service
- **Analysis** - Review your investing patterns
- **Deletion** - If you want us to delete it after export

## Delete After Export

If you want to delete your account after exporting:

1. Download your data
2. Verify the export is complete
3. [Delete your account →](/account/delete-account)

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How often can I request my data?

There's no limit, but please allow 30 days between requests to avoid system strain.

### What format is the data in?

Primarily JSON and CSV files. JSON for complex data, CSV for tabular data like watchlists.

### Is the data encrypted?

Your download link is secure and temporary. Once downloaded, the files are not encrypted—keep them safe.

### What if my export takes longer than expected?

Large accounts with extensive history may take up to 7 days. Contact support if you haven't received it after a week.

### Can I request specific data only?

Currently, we provide a complete export. You can filter the files after downloading.
