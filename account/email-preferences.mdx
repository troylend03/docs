# Email Preferences & Digest Settings

Control what emails you receive from Rallies and how often you receive them.

## Email Types

Rallies sends several types of emails:

| Type | Description | Can Unsubscribe? |
|------|-------------|------------------|
| **Transactional** | Receipts, password resets, security alerts | No |
| **Price Alerts** | Notifications when stocks hit your targets | Yes |
| **Earnings Alerts** | Upcoming earnings for watched stocks | Yes |
| **Daily Digest** | Summary of your watchlists | Yes |
| **Weekly Digest** | Weekly market summary | Yes |
| **Newsletter** | Product updates, tips, market insights | Yes |
| **Marketing** | Promotions and special offers | Yes |

## Managing Email Preferences

### On Web

1. Go to **Settings → Notifications** or **Settings → Email**
2. Toggle each email type on/off
3. Changes save automatically

### On Mobile

1. Open **Settings → Notifications**
2. Scroll to **Email** section
3. Toggle preferences as desired

### From Email Footer

Every marketing email includes an unsubscribe link:

1. Scroll to the bottom of any Rallies email
2. Click **"Unsubscribe"** or **"Manage Preferences"**
3. Adjust your settings

## Digest Settings

### Daily Digest

Receive a morning summary of your watchlists:

- **Included:** Prices, daily change, any triggered alerts
- **Timing:** Sent around 7 AM in your timezone
- **Toggle:** Settings → Email → Daily Digest

### Weekly Digest

Get a weekly summary every Sunday:

- **Included:** Week's performance, top movers, upcoming earnings
- **Timing:** Sent Sunday evening
- **Toggle:** Settings → Email → Weekly Digest

### Digest Customization

Customize what's in your digests:

1. Go to **Settings → Email → Digest Content**
2. Select which watchlists to include
3. Choose what data points matter most
4. Save preferences

## Alert Email Settings

For price alerts and earnings notifications:

### Delivery Options

- **Instant:** Get emails immediately when alerts trigger
- **Batched:** Receive hourly or daily batches instead of instant
- **Off:** Disable email alerts (still available in-app)

### Setting Alert Preferences

1. Go to **Settings → Notifications → Alerts**
2. Choose **Email**, **Push**, **Both**, or **Neither**
3. Select instant or batched delivery

## Unsubscribing from All Marketing

To stop all non-essential emails:

1. Go to **Settings → Email**
2. Click **"Unsubscribe from All"** (if available)
3. Or toggle off each category individually

Note: You'll still receive transactional emails (receipts, security alerts, password resets).

## Re-Subscribing

Changed your mind? Re-enable emails anytime:

1. Go to **Settings → Email**
2. Toggle on the email types you want
3. Or click links in your account to re-subscribe

## Troubleshooting

### Not receiving emails?

- Check your spam/junk folder
- Add `@rallies.ai` to your contacts
- Verify your email address in Settings
- [More troubleshooting →](/troubleshooting/not-receiving-emails)

### Getting too many emails?

- Switch from instant to batched alerts
- Reduce the number of price alerts
- Turn off daily digest if weekly is enough

### Emails going to spam?

- Mark Rallies emails as "Not Spam"
- Add `noreply@rallies.ai` and `alerts@rallies.ai` to contacts
- Check if your email provider has strict filtering

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I get alerts by email only (no push)?

Yes. Go to Settings → Notifications and enable email while disabling push for each alert type.

### Can I change what time digests are sent?

Digest timing is based on your timezone setting. Update your timezone in Settings → Account if needed.

### Why do I still get some emails after unsubscribing?

Transactional emails (receipts, password resets, security) cannot be unsubscribed from. These are essential for account function.

### Can I get digests for only certain watchlists?

Yes, in digest settings you can select which watchlists to include.
