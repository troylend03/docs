## What the Report Includes

### 1. Executive Summary

A quick overview covering:
- What the company does
- Current stock situation
- Key investment considerations

### 2. Business Analysis

- Business model explanation
- Revenue streams breakdown
- Competitive advantages (moats)

### 3. Financial Deep Dive

**Profitability**
- Gross margin trends
- Operating margin analysis
- Net margin comparison to peers
- Return on equity (ROE) and return on assets (ROA)

**Growth**
- Revenue growth (historical and projected)
- Earnings growth trajectory
- Segment-level growth where applicable

**Balance Sheet**
- Debt levels and debt-to-equity

- Working capital health

**Cash Flow**
- Operating cash flow trends
- Free cash flow generation
- Cash conversion quality
- Capital allocation priorities

### 4. Valuation Analysis

- Current P/E ratio with context
- Price-to-sales and EV/EBITDA
- Comparison to 5-year historical averages
- Peer group valuation comparison
- PEG ratio for growth-adjusted value
- Fair value estimate range

### 5. Technical Picture

- Price trend analysis (short, medium, long-term)
- Key support and resistance levels
- Moving average positions
- Momentum indicators
- Volume analysis
- Chart pattern observations

### 6. Sentiment & Ownership

- Analyst consensus rating
- Average price target
- Recent rating changes
- Institutional ownership trends
- Insider buying/selling activity
- Short interest levels

### 7. News & Catalysts

- Recent significant news
- Upcoming earnings date

### 8. Bull vs. Bear Summary

**Bull Case**
- Top 3-5 reasons to be optimistic
- What could drive the stock higher
- Best-case scenario factors

**Bear Case**
- Top 3-5 risks and concerns
- What could hurt the stock
- Worst-case scenario factors

### 9. Key Takeaways

- Summary of most important findings
- Critical factors to monitor
- Questions you might want to explore further

## Troubleshooting

### "Deep Research is taking too long"

Reports typically complete in 30-90 seconds. If it's taking longer:
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing and running again
- Some complex companies may take slightly longer

### "I don't see the Deep Research option"

Deep Research is a Pro feature. If you don't see it:
- Confirm you have an active Pro subscription
- Try logging out and back in
- [Upgrade to Pro](/billing/upgrade) if you're on Free tier

### "The report seems incomplete"

Occasionally, data availability issues can affect completeness:
- Very new companies may have limited financial history
- Some international stocks have less coverage
- Try running the report again
