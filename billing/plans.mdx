# Understanding Our Subscription Plans

Rallies.ai offers two plans: a free tier to get you started, and Rallies Pro for unlimited access and advanced features. This article explains what's included in each and helps you choose the right option.

## Plans at a Glance

| | Free | Pro |
|---|------|-----|
| **Price** | $0 | $19/month or $99/year |
| **AI Queries** | Limited weekly | Unlimited |
| **Market Data** | 15-min delayed | Real-time |
| **Deep Research** | ✗ | ✓ |
| **Watchlists** | Up to 3 | Unlimited |
| **Price Alerts** | Up to 5 | Unlimited |
| **Portfolios** | 1 | Multiple |

## Free Plan

The Free plan gives you access to the core Rallies experience at no cost, forever.

### What's Included

**AI Research Assistant**
Ask questions about stocks, markets, and investing. You get a set number of queries per week that resets every Monday.

**Stock Research**
Access stock pages with financial data, charts, analyst ratings, earnings info, and more for thousands of securities.

**Vibe Screener (Basic)**
Find stocks using natural language with basic screening capabilities.

**Watchlists**
Create up to 3 watchlists to track stocks you're interested in.

**Price Alerts**
Set up to 5 price alerts to get notified when stocks hit your targets.

**Portfolio Tracking**
Connect one brokerage account or manually add holdings. See basic performance and allocation.

**AI Arena**
View AI model competition results and see which models are performing best.

### Limitations

- Weekly cap on AI queries
- 15-minute delayed market data
- Basic screener filters only
- No Deep Research Mode
- No data export
- Standard support response times

### Who It's For

The Free plan is great for:
- New investors learning the basics
- Casual users who research occasionally
- Anyone wanting to try Rallies before subscribing
- Users with simple tracking needs

## Rallies Pro

Pro removes all limits and unlocks the full power of Rallies for serious investors.

### What's Included

**Everything in Free, plus:**

**Unlimited AI Queries**
Ask as many questions as you want, whenever you want. No weekly caps, no waiting.

**Real-Time Market Data**
See live prices instead of 15-minute delayed quotes. Essential for active traders and timely research.

**Deep Research Mode**
Get comprehensive analysis reports on any stock. The AI runs 100+ analysis steps covering financials, technicals, sentiment, insider activity, and more—delivered in about 60 seconds.

**Unlimited Watchlists**
Create as many watchlists as you need to organize your research by theme, strategy, or any criteria.

**Unlimited Price Alerts**
Set alerts on any stock without caps. Never miss a price target.

**Multiple Portfolios**
Track multiple brokerage accounts or strategies separately. See them individually or combined.

**Advanced Portfolio Analytics**
Get deeper insights including risk analysis, sector concentration, correlation data, earnings exposure, and AI-powered portfolio review.

**Full AI Arena Access**
Follow specific AI models, see their detailed reasoning, and get notifications when they make trades.

**Data Export**
Download your watchlists, portfolio data, and research for use outside Rallies.

**Priority Support**
Get faster response times when you need help.

### Who It's For

Pro is designed for:
- Active investors who research frequently
- Anyone hitting the free query limit
- Users who need real-time data
- Investors tracking multiple accounts
- Power users who want Deep Research and advanced features

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I know if I need Pro?

Ask yourself:
- Do you hit the AI query limit regularly?
- Do you need real-time prices?
- Do you want Deep Research reports?
- Do you track multiple portfolios?

If you answered yes to any of these, Pro is probably worth it. If not, Free may be all you need.

### Can I try Pro before paying?

Yes! Start a 7-day free trial to test all Pro features. Your card won't be charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel anytime.

### What happens if I cancel Pro?

You keep Pro access until your current billing period ends, then your account moves to the Free tier. Your data is never deleted. [Learn more →](/billing/what-happens-after-cancel)

### Is the annual plan refundable?

If you're not satisfied within 14 days of a new subscription, contact support for a full refund. [Refund policy →](/billing/refund-policy)

### Do prices include tax?

Prices shown are before applicable taxes. Depending on your location, sales tax or VAT may be added at checkout.

### Are there discounts for students or nonprofits?

We may offer special pricing in certain cases. [Contact support](/support/contact) to inquire.

### Can my company pay for my subscription?

Yes, you can use a corporate card. For invoicing or purchase orders, [contact support](/support/contact).
