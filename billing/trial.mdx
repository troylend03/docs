# Free Trial Information

Curious about Rallies Pro? Our free trial lets you experience all premium features before committing.

## Trial Overview

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Length** | 7 days |
| **Features** | Full Pro access |
| **Payment required** | Yes, at signup |
| **Charged when** | After trial ends (if not cancelled) |
| **Cancel anytime** | Yes, no charge if cancelled before trial ends |

## What's Included in the Trial

During your 7-day trial, you get full access to:

- ✓ **Unlimited AI queries** - No daily limits
- ✓ **Real-time market data** - Live prices, not delayed
- ✓ **Deep Research mode** - Comprehensive AI analysis
- ✓ **Unlimited watchlists** - Track as many as you want
- ✓ **Unlimited price alerts** - Never miss a move
- ✓ **Full AI Arena access** - All models and features
- ✓ **Priority support** - Faster response times

Essentially, everything a Pro subscriber gets.

## Starting a Trial

### On Web

1. Click **"Start Free Trial"** or **"Try Pro Free"**
2. Enter your payment information
3. Your 7-day trial begins immediately
4. You won't be charged unless the trial ends

### On Mobile (iOS)

1. Tap **"Try Pro"** or go to **Settings → Subscription**
2. Select the Pro plan with trial
3. Confirm with Face ID/Touch ID via Apple
4. Trial starts immediately

### On Mobile (Android)

1. Tap **"Try Pro"** or go to **Settings → Subscription**
2. Select the Pro plan with trial
3. Confirm via Google Play
4. Trial starts immediately

## Why Do You Need Payment Info?

We collect payment information upfront so that:

- Your experience isn't interrupted if you want to continue
- The transition to paid is seamless
- You don't have to re-enter details later

**You won't be charged** during the trial. If you cancel before day 7, you pay nothing.

## When Will I Be Charged?

You're charged on **day 8** if you don't cancel:

- **Monthly plan:** Full monthly rate
- **Annual plan:** Full annual rate

You'll receive an email reminder before your trial ends.

## How to Cancel Your Trial

To avoid being charged, cancel before your trial ends:

### On Web
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **"Cancel Trial"** or **"Cancel Subscription"**
3. Confirm cancellation

### On iOS
1. Go to **iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions**
2. Find Rallies and tap **"Cancel"**

### On Android
1. Go to **Play Store → Subscriptions**
2. Find Rallies and tap **"Cancel"**

You'll keep Pro access until the trial period ends.

## After Your Trial Ends

### If you don't cancel:
- You're charged automatically
- Pro access continues without interruption
- You become a regular Pro subscriber

### If you cancel:
- You keep Pro features until day 7
- On day 8, you revert to the free tier
- You're not charged anything
- Your data is preserved

## Trial Eligibility

Free trials are limited to:

- **One trial per person** - Based on account/payment method
- **New customers only** - Former Pro subscribers aren't eligible
- **Subject to availability** - Trial offers may change

If you've had a trial before, you'll see regular pricing instead.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I extend my trial?

Trials can't be extended, but contact support if you have questions about whether Pro is right for you.

### What if I forget to cancel?

If you're charged after your trial and contact us within 48 hours, we'll typically issue a refund. [Contact support →](/support/contact)

### Can I switch from monthly to annual during the trial?

Yes, you can change your plan selection before the trial ends in your billing settings.

### Will I get another trial if I cancel and come back later?

No, trials are one-time offers. When you return, you'll subscribe at the regular price.

### Is the trial really free?

Yes. If you cancel before day 7, you pay $0. We only charge if you continue past the trial.
