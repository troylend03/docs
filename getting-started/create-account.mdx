# Creating Your Account

Get started with Rallies.ai in under a minute. You can sign up with your email address or use Google or Apple for one-click account creation.

## Sign Up on Web

### Option 1: Email Sign Up

1. **Go to [rallies.ai](https://rallies.ai)**

2. **Click "Sign Up"** in the top right corner

3. **Enter your information:**
   - Email address
   - Password (at least 8 characters)

4. **Click "Create Account"**

5. **Check your email** for a verification link and click it to activate your account

### Option 2: Sign Up with Google

1. **Go to [rallies.ai](https://rallies.ai)**

2. **Click "Sign Up"**

3. **Click "Continue with Google"**

4. **Select your Google account** or sign in to Google if prompted

5. **You're in!** No email verification neededâ€”your account is ready to use immediately.

### Option 3: Sign Up with Apple

1. **Go to [rallies.ai](https://rallies.ai)**

2. **Click "Sign Up"**

3. **Click "Continue with Apple"**

4. **Sign in with your Apple ID**

5. **Choose whether to share or hide your email:**
   - **Share My Email:** Rallies sees your real email address
   - **Hide My Email:** Apple creates a private relay address

6. **You're in!** No additional verification needed.

## Sign Up on iOS

1. **Download the Rallies app** from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/rallies)

2. **Open the app** and tap "Sign Up"

3. **Choose your sign-up method:**
   - **Email:** Enter your email and create a password
   - **Continue with Apple:** One tap using Face ID or Touch ID
   - **Continue with Google:** Sign in with your Google account

4. **If using email,** check your inbox for a verification link

## Sign Up on Android

1. **Download the Rallies app** from the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=ai.rallies)

2. **Open the app** and tap "Sign Up"

3. **Choose your sign-up method:**
   - **Email:** Enter your email and create a password
   - **Continue with Google:** One tap using your Google account
   - **Continue with Apple:** Sign in with your Apple ID (if you have one)

4. **If using email,** check your inbox for a verification link

## Password Requirements

If you're creating an account with email, your password must:

- Be at least 8 characters long
- We recommend including a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols

## Troubleshooting

**"This email is already registered"**

You may have created an account previously. Try:
- [Resetting your password â†’](/account/reset-password)
- Signing in with Google or Apple if you used social login before

**"Invalid email address"**

Make sure your email:
- Doesn't have typos
- Includes the @ symbol and domain (e.g., @gmail.com)
- Doesn't have extra spaces before or after

**"Password doesn't meet requirements"**

Your password needs to be at least 8 characters. Try making it longer or adding numbers and symbols.

**Google/Apple sign-in not working**

- Make sure pop-ups are enabled in your browser
- Try disabling browser extensions that might block sign-in windows
- Clear your browser cache and try again
