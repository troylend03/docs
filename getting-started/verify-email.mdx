# Verifying Your Email

When you create a Rallies account with your email address, we send a verification link to confirm it's really you. This keeps your account secure and ensures you receive important notifications.

## How to Verify Your Email

1. **Check your inbox** for an email from Rallies.ai with the subject "Verify your email address"

2. **Open the email** and click the **"Verify Email"** button

3. **You're verified!** You'll be redirected to Rallies and can start using all features.

The verification link is valid for 24 hours. If it expires, you can request a new one.

## Resend the Verification Email

If you didn't receive the email or the link expired:

### On Web

1. Go to [rallies.ai](https://rallies.ai) and sign in with your email and password
2. You'll see a banner saying "Please verify your email"
3. Click **"Resend verification email"**
4. Check your inbox (and spam folder)

### On Mobile

1. Open the Rallies app and sign in
2. Tap the verification reminder banner
3. Tap **"Resend Email"**
4. Check your inbox

## Didn't Receive the Email?

If the verification email isn't in your inbox after a few minutes, try these steps:

### 1. Check Your Spam or Junk Folder

Verification emails sometimes get filtered. Look in:
- Spam
- Junk
- Promotions (Gmail)
- Other/Focused (Outlook)

If you find it there, mark it as "Not Spam" so future Rallies emails reach your inbox.

### 2. Check the Email Address

Make sure you signed up with the correct email. Common issues:
- Typos (gamil.com instead of gmail.com)
- Wrong domain (.com vs .net)
- Using a different email than intended

To check which email your account uses, try signing inâ€”the login error message will confirm if the email exists in our system.

### 3. Add Rallies to Your Contacts

Add **hello@rallies.ai** to your email contacts or address book. This helps prevent future emails from being filtered.

### 4. Check Email Filters

If you have custom email rules or filters, make sure they're not automatically moving or deleting emails from rallies.ai.

### 5. Wait a Few Minutes

Sometimes email delivery takes up to 10 minutes, especially with certain email providers. If you just requested a resend, give it a bit of time.

### 6. Try a Different Email

If you consistently can't receive emails at your current address, you may need to create a new account with a different email, or sign up using Google or Apple instead.

## Corporate or School Email Issues

Some corporate and educational email systems have strict spam filters that may block emails from new senders.

**Try these solutions:**

- Ask your IT department to whitelist emails from @rallies.ai
- Use a personal email address (Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, etc.) instead
- Sign up with Google or Apple to bypass email verification entirely

## Verification Link Expired

Verification links expire after 24 hours for security reasons. If your link has expired:

1. Go to [rallies.ai](https://rallies.ai)
2. Sign in with your email and password
3. Click **"Resend verification email"** on the banner
4. A fresh verification link will be sent to your inbox

## Why We Require Email Verification

Email verification helps us:

- **Confirm your identity** - Ensures the account belongs to you
- **Protect your account** - Prevents unauthorized signups using your email
- **Deliver important notifications** - Password resets, alerts, and security notices go to a verified address
- **Enable account recovery** - If you ever lose access, we can help you recover it through your verified email

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I use Rallies without verifying my email?

You can sign in and explore, but some features may be limited until you verify. We recommend verifying as soon as possible to ensure you receive important notifications like price alerts and security updates.

### I verified but still see the verification banner

Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page (or close and reopen the app)
2. Sign out and sign back in
3. Clear your browser cache

If the banner persists after these steps, [contact support](/support/contact).

### Can I change my email before verifying?

Yes, you can update your email address in Settings before verifying. The verification email will then be sent to your new address. [Learn how to change your email â†’](/account/change-email)

### The verification link says it's invalid

This usually means:
- The link has expired (after 24 hours)
- The link was truncated (try copying the full URL)
- You've already verified (try signing in normally)

Request a fresh verification email by signing in and clicking "Resend."
