# Downloading the Mobile App

Get Rallies on your phone for investment research anywhere. The mobile app gives you full access to the AI Research Assistant, portfolio tracking, watchlists, and price alerts—all optimized for your phone.

## Download for iPhone & iPad

### From the App Store

1. Open the **App Store** on your iPhone or iPad
2. Tap the **Search** tab
3. Type **"Rallies"** or **"Rallies.ai"**
4. Find the Rallies app (look for our logo)
5. Tap **"Get"** or the download icon
6. Authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password
7. Wait for the download to complete
8. Open the app and sign in (or create an account)

### Direct Link

[Download Rallies for iOS →](https://apps.apple.com/app/rallies)

Or scan this QR code with your iPhone camera:

[QR Code placeholder - insert actual QR code in production]

### Requirements

- iOS 15.0 or later
- iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch
- About 100 MB of storage space

## Download for Android

### From Google Play

1. Open the **Google Play Store** on your Android device
2. Tap the **search bar**
3. Type **"Rallies"** or **"Rallies.ai"**
4. Find the Rallies app in results
5. Tap **"Install"**
6. Wait for download and installation
7. Tap **"Open"**
8. Sign in or create an account

### Direct Link

[Download Rallies for Android →](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=ai.rallies)

### Requirements

- Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later
- About 100 MB of storage space
- Google Play Services

## After Installing

### Sign In

If you already have a Rallies account:

1. Open the app
2. Tap **"Sign In"**
3. Enter your email and password
   - Or tap **"Continue with Google"** / **"Continue with Apple"**
4. Complete any two-factor authentication if enabled
5. You're in! Your watchlists, portfolio, and settings sync automatically.

### Create an Account

If you're new to Rallies:

1. Open the app
2. Tap **"Sign Up"**
3. Choose to sign up with email, Google, or Apple
4. Follow the prompts to create your account
5. Start researching!

[Detailed account creation guide →](/getting-started/create-account)

### Enable Notifications

To receive price alerts and important updates:

**iOS:**
When prompted, tap **"Allow"** to enable notifications. You can also enable later:
1. Go to iPhone **Settings**
2. Scroll to **Rallies**
3. Tap **Notifications**
4. Toggle **Allow Notifications** on

**Android:**
Notifications are typically enabled by default. To check:
1. Go to phone **Settings**
2. Tap **Apps** → **Rallies**
3. Tap **Notifications**
4. Make sure notifications are enabled

## Syncing Between Devices

Your Rallies account syncs across all devices:

- **Watchlists** created on web appear on mobile, and vice versa
- **Portfolio** data is the same everywhere
- **Settings** sync automatically
- **Conversations** with the AI are available on any device

Sign in with the same account on all devices to keep everything in sync.

## Troubleshooting Download Issues

### "App not available in your country"

The Rallies app may not be available in all countries yet. Check back later or use the web version at [rallies.ai](https://rallies.ai) in the meantime.

### "Incompatible with your device"

Your device may not meet minimum requirements:
- iOS: Requires iOS 15.0+
- Android: Requires Android 8.0+

Try updating your device's operating system if possible.

### "Not enough storage"

The app needs about 100 MB. Free up space by:
- Deleting unused apps
- Clearing photo/video cache
- Removing old messages or downloads

### Download stuck or failed

1. Check your internet connection
2. Restart the App Store or Play Store
3. Restart your phone
4. Try downloading on WiFi instead of cellular
5. Sign out and back into your Apple ID/Google account

### Can't find the app in search

- Try searching "Rallies" without ".ai"
- Check that you're in the correct store (App Store for iOS, Play Store for Android)
- The app may be new to your region—try the direct links above

## Updating the App

Keep the app updated for the best experience and latest features.

### iOS
- App Store → Your profile icon → Scroll to see available updates → Update Rallies
- Or enable automatic updates: Settings → App Store → App Updates ON

### Android
- Play Store → Profile icon → Manage apps → Updates available → Update Rallies
- Or enable auto-update: Play Store → Settings → Auto-update apps

## Deleting the App

If you need to remove the app:

### iOS
- Press and hold the Rallies app icon
- Tap **"Remove App"** → **"Delete App"**

### Android
- Press and hold the app icon
- Drag to **"Uninstall"** or tap **"Uninstall"**

**Note:** Deleting the app doesn't delete your account or data. Your watchlists, portfolio, and settings remain safe. Reinstall anytime and sign back in to restore everything.

To actually delete your account and data, see [Deleting Your Account →](/account/delete-account)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is the mobile app free?

Yes, the app is free to download. Your Rallies subscription (Free or Pro) applies across web and mobile—no separate mobile subscription needed.

### Do I need an account to use the app?

Yes, you need to sign in or create an account to use the app.

### Can I use the app without internet?

Limited functionality. You can view previously loaded data, but AI queries, real-time prices, and syncing require an internet connection.

### Will the app drain my battery?

The app is optimized for efficiency. Normal use shouldn't significantly impact battery. Background refresh for alerts uses minimal power.

### Is there a tablet version?

The iOS app works on iPad with an optimized interface. On Android, the app works on tablets though the interface is phone-optimized.
