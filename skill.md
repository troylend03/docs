# Rallies Support Center Skill

This skill helps users get support for Rallies.ai, an AI-powered investment research platform that provides stock analysis, portfolio tracking, and AI-driven insights.

## What Rallies.ai Does

Rallies.ai is an investment research and educational platform (not a financial advisor) that offers:

- **AI Research Assistant**: Ask questions in plain English about stocks, markets, and investing
- **Stock Analysis Tools**: Financial statements, charts, analyst ratings, earnings data, SEC filings
- **Vibe Screener**: Find stocks using natural language (e.g., "undervalued tech stocks with strong cash flow")
- **Watchlists & Price Alerts**: Track stocks and get notified at target prices
- **Portfolio Tracking**: Connect brokerages or manually add holdings for performance analysis
- **AI Arena**: Watch AI models compete in simulated stock-picking competitions

## Constraints

### What Rallies CANNOT Do
- Provide personalized buy/sell recommendations
- Give financial, tax, or legal advice
- Predict stock prices
- Replace a financial advisor
- Execute real trades

### Important Disclaimers
- Rallies.ai is NOT a registered investment advisor
- All content is for educational and informational purposes only
- Users are responsible for their own investment decisions
- AI Arena uses simulated portfolios (no real money)
- Past performance does not guarantee future results

## Subscription Tiers

### Free Tier

- Basic AI Arena access

### Pro Tier
- Unlimited AI queries
- Real-time market data

The platform pulls from multiple financial data providers:
- Real-time and historical stock prices
- Financial statements (income, balance sheet, cash flow)
- Analyst ratings and price targets
- SEC filings (10-K, 10-Q, 8-K)
- Institutional holdings (13F filings)

## Workflows

### New User Setup
1. Create account with email, Google, or Apple
2. Verify email address
3. Ask first AI question
4. Create a watchlist
5. (Optional) Connect brokerage or add holdings manually

### Troubleshoot Login Issues
1. Check email address is correct
2. Try password reset if forgotten
3. Check for verification email in spam
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try different browser or incognito mode
6. Contact support if issues persist

### Cancel Subscription
1. Go to Settings > Subscription
2. Click "Cancel subscription"
3. For mobile: cancel through App Store/Google Play
4. Access continues until billing period ends
5. Data is preserved on Free tier

### Report AI Issue
1. Click thumbs down on problematic response
2. Describe what was wrong
3. Provide correct information if known
4. Submit feedback
5. For recurring issues, email support@rallies.ai
